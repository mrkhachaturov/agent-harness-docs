# Student discount

Cursor discontinued new sign-ups for the legacy student discount on June 25, 2026.

## Why did Cursor discontinue the student discount?

Cursor stopped new sign-ups for the legacy student discount. The program had become a target for fraud and held Cursor back from reaching students globally. If you already redeemed your student discount, you'll keep your current rate until your plan expires.

Students still have access to discounts and credits through on-campus and online events this fall. [Sign up for our campus newsletter](https://anysphere.typeform.com/to/Xq2p21jK) to get early access to upcoming events, exclusive discounts, and beginner guides.

## What happened to the student discount page?

The student discount is no longer available, and there are new ways to get access to Cursor:

- Undergraduates can claim credits and discounts at on-campus and online events starting this fall.
- Master's students, PhD candidates, academic researchers, and educators can [fill out this form](https://anysphere.typeform.com/to/bgTJGDeA) to request credits to support their work.

[Sign up for our campus newsletter](https://anysphere.typeform.com/to/Xq2p21jK) to get early access to upcoming events, exclusive discounts, and beginner guides.

## How can I get access to Cursor as a student now?

Undergraduates can claim credits and discounts at on-campus and online events starting this fall. Master's students, PhD candidates, academic researchers, and educators can [fill out this form](https://anysphere.typeform.com/to/bgTJGDeA) to request credits to support their work.

[Sign up for our campus newsletter](https://anysphere.typeform.com/to/Xq2p21jK) to get early access to upcoming events, exclusive discounts, and beginner guides.

## I'm mid-subscription on the student discount. Does my rate hold?

Yes. If you already redeemed your student discount, you'll keep your current rate until your plan expires.

## What happens after the student discount year ends?

Your Pro subscription continues at the regular $20/month rate unless you cancel.
